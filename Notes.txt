Roguelike

Pond areas, Forest areas, Mine areas. Some at home base to practice, then you also craft along the way.

Learn to craft your own things, or find loot, or buy things from shops

Embark on "quests" that will be a crawl with different skill checks, a la Underground Pass. Also monk island in RS where you go make your own things there.

Can take different routes while there

Equip different parts of your body with loot

Using those body parts/skills/equipment builds your skill, and can learn permanent things from them

Equipment synergy with skills, ie. materia

You go deeper into the caveas you pass more skill checks. Those skill checks gotta be interesting.

At the end of the Quest, you have to use your skills to obtain shards... and then you return and can use those shards to do things.
